# QuestFit Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in QuestFit a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body type, disability, experience, identity, nationality, race, religion, or other personal characteristics.

Fitness is personal. Discussions about bodies, ability, health, and movement must remain considerate and must never shame, diagnose, or pressure another participant.

## Expected Behavior

- Communicate with empathy, patience, and respect.
- Give constructive feedback about the work, not the person.
- Respect different bodies, abilities, fitness backgrounds, and accessibility needs.
- Protect personal, health, camera, account, and security information.
- Accept responsibility, apologize when appropriate, and learn from mistakes.
- Prioritize community safety and the project's privacy boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, or personal attacks.
- Body shaming, medical claims about another person, or dismissing accessibility needs.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Sharing webcam images, raw pose data, credentials, or sensitive vulnerability details.
- Deliberate disruption, intimidation, or sustained bad-faith participation.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, demonstrations, community spaces, and public interactions where someone represents QuestFit.

## Reporting and Enforcement

For a conduct concern, open a minimal repository issue requesting a private contact channel. Do not include private, identifying, security, health, or incident details in the public issue.

Maintainers will review reports in good faith, protect the reporter's privacy where possible, and respond proportionately. Actions may include a private warning, content removal, temporary participation restrictions, or a permanent ban. Maintainers must recuse themselves when they have a conflict of interest.

## Attribution

This project-specific code of conduct is informed by common open-source community standards and tailored to QuestFit's fitness, privacy, and safety context.
